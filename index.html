Pitch Scripts & Objection Handling

Opening Script (15-20 seconds)
"Good [morning/afternoon], I'm [Name] from [Company]. We're in the area helping homeowners like yourself reduce their electricity bills by £150-300 per month with a fully-maintained solar and battery system - completely free to install. Do you own this property?"

If YES: "Brilliant. Have you looked into solar before, or is this something new?"

If NO (renter): "No problem at all. Do you know if your landlord might be interested? [Get details or move on]"

Quick Pre-Qualification (30 seconds)
  1. "Do you own this property?" (MUST be yes)
  2. "Is your roof south-facing, or does it get good sun?" (Ideal: south/SW/SE, pitched)
  3. "What's your electricity bill like at the moment?" (Higher = better fit)

Disqualify if: Renter, flat, heavy shading, north-facing only, recent solar installation

Devon Rural Pain Points
Pain Point Pitch Angle
Off-gas electric heating "You're paying premium rates to heat your home. This system cuts that dramatically."
High winter bills "Imagine your January bill being half what it was - that's what we're seeing."
Oil price volatility "Unlike oil, the sun doesn't have price shocks. Lock in savings for 25 years."
Energy independence "With batteries, you're covered even during power cuts - common in rural areas."
House value "Solar adds 4-14% to property value according to recent studies."
Objection Handling
Objection Response
"I'm too old / won't be here 25 years" "The lease transfers with the property - it's actually a selling point for buyers. And you'll see savings from day one."
"What's the catch?" "Fair question. We get paid by Hometree, not you. Your only commitment is the monthly lease, which is fixed to rise only 2.5% per year - less than inflation."
"I need to think about it" "Absolutely. Can I book a 30-minute appointment for our technical team to show you exactly what you'd save? No obligation, just the numbers."
"I've had bad experiences with solar salespeople" "I understand. This isn't about pushy sales - it's a straightforward lease. You'll meet our technical team who'll design the system properly."
"Already have solar" "Great choice! Do you have batteries? We can often add storage to existing systems. Also - know any neighbours who might be interested?"
Closing for Appointment
"Based on what you've told me, you'd be a great fit. Our technical supervisor can visit this week or next to design your system and show you the exact savings. Would [Day] morning or afternoon work better?"
